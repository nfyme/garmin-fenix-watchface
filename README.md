Minimalist dark face packed with data.

At a glance: time, battery, Bluetooth status, sunrise/sunset, moon phase with real illumination rendering.

Weather block shows current conditions plus 3h and 6h forecast with temperature, wind speed and direction.

Outer precipitation ring visualizes hourly rain and snow probability around the clock face.

Bottom block is switchable between monthly calendar (with weekend highlighting) and activity stats.

Settings: weather refresh interval, weekend color, bottom block mode.
