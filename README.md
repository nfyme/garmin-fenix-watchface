Minimalist dark face packed with data.

At a glance: time, battery, Bluetooth status, sunrise/sunset, moon phase with real illumination rendering and days-to-next-phase counter.

Weather block shows current conditions plus 3h and 6h forecast with temperature, wind speed and direction. Choose between Garmin's built-in weather or Open-Meteo (GPS or Garmin Weather location) for more frequent, independent updates.

Outer precipitation ring visualizes hourly rain/snow probability and intensity around the clock face, with an optional danger ring for storms, hail and other severe conditions.

Under the weather block: steps progress bar or barometric pressure trend (hPa/mmHg), your choice.

Bottom block is switchable between monthly calendar (with weekend highlighting) and activity stats (steps, calories, floors, heart rate).

Extensive color settings for hour/minute/colon, weekend days, progress bars, and more.
